Research

I am interested in a variety of topics related to galaxies, hydrodynamics, and computational astrophysics. My dissertation focuses on the physics of starburst-driven galactic winds, coupled with the creation of a new GPU-based hydrodynamics code, Cholla, which I wrote from scratch. Cholla was designed to be massively-parallel and extremely efficient, and it has been tested on some of the largest supercomputers in the world. Click on the pics below for links to my research blog, the Cholla download page (the code is free and open-source), and Cholla reference paper.
